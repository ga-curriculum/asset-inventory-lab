## Scenario

It's time to put the finishing touches on your updated inventory and prepare a final report for your manager. This report will summarize the work you've done, highlight the key improvements in the inventory, and provide recommendations for maintaining accurate asset data going forward.

### Exercise Steps

1. Start by carefully reviewing your updated [General_Legacy_Inventory.xlsx](../General_Legacy_Inventory.xlsx) file:

   - Ensure all necessary data fields are populated for all assets.
   - Double-check that the data is consistent and properly formatted across all records.
   - Confirm that all issues identified in the "Inventory Issues" worksheet have been addressed or noted for follow-up.

2. If you identify any remaining gaps or inconsistencies during your review, address them in the inventory and update the "Inventory Issues" worksheet accordingly.

3. Once you're confident in the completeness and accuracy of the updated inventory, create a new document named "Inventory Update Report" to summarize your work.

4. In the report, include the following sections:

   - **Introduction:** Briefly explain the objectives and scope of the inventory update project.

   - **Methodology:** Describe the steps you took to reconcile the legacy data, integrate new information, and incorporate intangible assets. Reference the "Inventory Issues" worksheet as a key part of your process.

   - **Results:** Highlight the key improvements in the updated inventory compared to the legacy data. This might include the number of assets added, data fields populated, inconsistencies resolved, etc.

   - **Recommendations:** Provide suggestions for maintaining the accuracy of the inventory going forward. This might include regular data audits, processes for updating the inventory as assets change, or training for staff on proper asset data management.

   - **Conclusion:** Summarize the overall impact of the updated inventory on General Corporation's IT asset management capabilities and potential next steps.

5. Append the final version of your [General_Legacy_Inventory.xlsx](../General_Legacy_Inventory.xlsx) file (including all worksheets) to the report.

### Deliverables

- A final, validated version of the [General_Legacy_Inventory.xlsx](../General_Legacy_Inventory.xlsx) file, reflecting all updates and additions made throughout the exercises.

- An "Inventory Update Report" document summarizing your work, results, and recommendations.
